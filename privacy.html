Privacy Policy for Lia

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lia - Learning Intelligence Assessment ("Lia", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy applies to the Lia chatbot, a service provided by Andrew Atef. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Our chatbot, Lia, is designed to have a natural conversation with you to understand your learning needs and suggest suitable courses.

2. Information We Collect

To provide personalized course recommendations, Lia needs to understand your preferences. We collect the following types of information through your conversation with the bot:

  • Learning Goals: The topics, skills, or subjects you express interest in learning (e.g., "Python for data analysis," "digital marketing," "learning French").
  • Budget Information: The financial range you are willing to spend on a course (e.g., "under $50," "free courses," "around 100 EGP").
  • Language Preference: The language in which you prefer to take the course (e.g., "English," "Arabic").
  • Conversation History: The full transcript of your interaction with Lia. This helps us improve the bot's understanding and response quality.
  • Usage Data: We may automatically collect technical information, such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamps of your interaction. This data is used for analytics, security, and service improvement.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information like your full name, email address, or payment details unless you voluntarily provide it within the chat.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Personalized Recommendations: The primary use of your learning goals, budget, and language preference is to filter and suggest the most relevant courses for you.
  • To Improve Our Service: We analyze conversation history and usage data to understand how users interact with Lia, identify areas for improvement, and enhance the bot's AI and natural language processing capabilities.
  • To Maintain Security and Functionality: Technical data helps us monitor the service for security threats, debug issues, and ensure the bot operates correctly.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We value your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share information under the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may use third-party companies for hosting and analytics. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated to protect your data.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by public authorities.

When Lia suggests a course, it will provide a link to a third-party course provider. This Privacy Policy does not cover the data practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information. You may request to:

  • Access a copy of your conversation history.
  • Request the deletion of your conversation history.

To make such a request, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Lia is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
